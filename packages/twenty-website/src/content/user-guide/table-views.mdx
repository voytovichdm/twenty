# Filter and Sort

Simplify Person management with filters:

-  Click **Filter** > select a field (Name, Company, Role, Creation Date, etc.).
-  Choose a condition, select a value, and apply the filter.
-  Add more filters with `+ Add filter` or remove them with **X**.

Sort People by Name, Company, or Creation Date:

-  Click **Sort**, choose Ascending/Descending, and select a field.

### Field display

You can customize the fields that ared displayed within a view. To hide a field, you can either click **Options** > **Fields** and select the `-` button next to the field or click on the field column header and select **hide**.

You can rearrange fields by clicking their field column header and then press **Move Right** or **Move Left**.

# Adding a custom field

To create a custom field for the **People** object, navigate to **Settings** > **Data Model** > **People**. Click on **Add Field**. Choose a field name and type. The new field will be available in the app. Alternatively, use the **+** button in the top right of the People table and select **Customize fields**.


# Add Companies
You can add as many Companies as you need, without any limitations. To add a Company, just click on the **+** button located at the top right of the screen or at the top of the Companies **Name** column. 


Enter the company name and domain name then press `Enter` to save. If you need to edit a Company name, click on it in the Company detail page.

# Delete a Company

**Index View:** To delete a Company, select the checkbox next to the Company record and click the delete button located below.

**Record Page:** Tap on the **3 dots menu** in the top right corner, and select delete from there.

# Filter and Sort

Make Company management a breeze with filters:

-   Click **Filter** > select a field (Name, Industry, Creation Date, etc.).
-   Choose a condition, select a value, and apply the filter.
-   Increase specificity with `+ Add filter` or remove unnecessary filters with **X**.

Sort Companies (by Name, Industry, or Creation Date, etc.):

-   Click on **Sort**, choose Ascending/Descending, and select a field.

### Field display

You have the option to customize the fields displayed in each view. To hide a field, click on **Options** > **Fields**, and select the `-` button next to it, or click on the field column header and choose **hide**.

While personalizing your view, you can also rearrange fields. Click on a specific field column header, and then press **Move Right** or **Move Left**.

# Adding a custom field

To add a custom field for the **Companies** object, go to **Settings** > **Data Model** > **Companies**. Click on **Add Field**. Choose a field name and type. Your new field will then be available within the app. For a quicker route, use the **+** button in the top right of the Companies table and select **Customize fields**.
